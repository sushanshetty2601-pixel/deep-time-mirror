# 🌍 Deep-Time Mirror

### *Piercing the veil of time for Earth Day 2026.*

**Deep-Time Mirror** is an immersive, AI-powered ecological portal that visualizes the environmental history and restored future of any location on Earth. Built for the Earth Day Challenge, it combines high-performance 3D graphics with real-time generative storytelling to bridge the gap between our primeval past and a sustainable solar-punk tomorrow.

---

## ⚡ The Experience

### 🔭 The Deep-Time Mirror
1. **Temporal Synthesis:** The AI stabilization engine kicks in, generating a cinematic, full-screen 3D visual of your location.
2. **Deep Past (~10,000 BCE):** Witness the land in its untouched, primeval glory. Meet ancient species and see the world before the Anthropocene.
3. **The Present:** A gritty, realistic look at current ecological challenges, complete with a "Recent Pulse" that grabs local environmental data from 1 day and 1 week ago.
4. **Restored Future (~2100 CE):** A solar-punk vision of restoration. Witness how architecture and nature can coexist in a vibrant, clean-energy ecosystem.

### 🧠 The Earth Auditor (Gaia Corp)
*   **Brutally Honest Audit:** Confess your daily habits to a high-end AI "Earth" that isn't afraid to tell the truth.
*   **Planetary Stability Score:** Receive a data-driven score (0-100) and an Earth Grade (A+ to F).
*   **Impact Radar:** Visualize your lifestyle across 5 dimensions: Energy, Transport, Waste, Water, and Diet.
*   **Performance Improvement Plan (PIP):** Get actionable, metric-backed tips (e.g., *Saves ~4.2kg CO2*) to restore your relationship with the biosphere.

## 🛠️ The Tech Stack

- **Core Engine:** [React 19](https://react.dev/) + [Vite](https://vitejs.dev/)
- **Artificial Intelligence:** 
  - **Google Gemini 1.5 Flash:** Powers the temporal narratives, ecological stats, "Google Search" grounded real-time data, and the brutally honest Auditor personality.
- **3D Graphics:** [Three.js](https://threejs.org/) via `@react-three/fiber` and `@react-three/drei`.
- **Data Visualization:** [Recharts](https://recharts.org/) for multi-dimensional radar charts and responsive impact dials.
- **Styling & UI:** [Tailwind CSS v4](https://tailwindcss.com/) with a custom luxury/editorial theme.
- **Motion:** [Framer Motion](https://www.framer.com/motion/) for cinematic epoch transitions.

---

## 🔬 How it Works

The app utilizes **LLM-driven temporal grounding**. When a user searches for a location:
1. **Gemini Search:** The AI fetches real-time ecological reports and historical climate data.
2. **Visual Synthesis:** It creates detailed visual descriptions to drive the immersive background rendering.
3. **Structured Temporal Data:** The system parses complex ecological facts into JSON-structured epochs, ensuring factual consistency while maintaining a poetic narrative.
4. **Behavioral Analysis:** The Earth Auditor uses sentiment analysis to penalize harmful habits (like littering) with failing grades and low scores.
